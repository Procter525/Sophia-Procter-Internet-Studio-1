Favorite Films

I could not decide which movies I genuinely wanted to put as my favorites, so what I decided to do instead was pick a movie from the 12 Basic Film Genres list that I found. My initial idea was to put all of the Star Wars films though, so it's probably worth mentioning that I love those films. As a backup plan I was going to include every Middle Earth related film, so probably also worth another mention.
