Meet Your Type: A Field Guide to Love and Typography: What are different ways to establish hierarchy (or emphasis) in typography design?

Establishing hiearchy can be done by putting information in the order of importance, which is commonly executed by ordering the information physically based on the language, for instance, in English people read top to bottom, so if making a list put the important information on the top. Another way to order importance is to show the important information in a way that makes it stand out, like making the important vocabulary bolded or underlined, or simply making it a headline and in bigger font.
