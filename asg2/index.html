Article Questions and Answers

What is the difference between structured content and semantic HTML?

Structured content is planned, developed and connected outside of an interface and if focused on small amounts of data that relates to one another whereas semantic HTML communicates information relating to relationships between document elements.

How do they work together?

When data is broken down into small chunks of information semantic HTML can then be used to show off this information in the form of information boxes on a search engine or simply grouped together on a website.

Why is it important to have structured content and semantic HTML in your web pages?

To group information together in a more clear fashion and to put related information together in an easily accessible format.

Hyperbolic discounting is the trendoncy to become more rational when that person is forced to wait, which is essentially procrastination. One method of countering hyperbolic discounting though is to trick your mind into thinking that the "now you" can't trust "future you" and therefore should do the work no instead of later, because "future you" will give in to the sidetracking temptations.
